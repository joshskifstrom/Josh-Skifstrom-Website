Josh Skifstrom is an acoustic singer/writer that enjoys playing on the Central Coast of California. He started playing at a young age joining a punk band that toured the United States playing in Vans Warped Tour.

Throughout the years, he's enjoyed being in a number of bands entertaining with covers and originals. You can Find him performing at various venues in San Luis Obispo county playing easy listening acoustic jams.
